The Biggest Football League in the America's:

Liga MX is the Football league in Mexico which is the biggest in its region, CONCACAF.
This league is divided into two small tournaments. The "Apertura", starts in July and ends in December, then the "Clausura" from January to May.
Liga MX was originally an amateur league back in the early 1920s to 30s. Then, the Federacion Mexicana de Futbol, or FMF announced the creation of a professional football leauge in Mexico in 1943, as the "Liga Mayor".
10 clubs were interested in joining this new leauge, America, Asturias, Atlante, Veracruz Sporting, Nexaca, and Marte from the Liga Mexicana de Football Amateur Association, Atlas and Guadalajara from the Liga Occidental de Jalisco, and ADO and Moctezuma from the Liga Amateur de Veracruz.
