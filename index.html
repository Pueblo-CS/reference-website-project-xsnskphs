Football is a sport where two teams of 11 players play on a pitch and try to kick a ball into a net and score goals.
It is the most popular sport in the world, with 3.5 billion fans.
Many people want to become a professional footballer, and represent their country, in the biggest tournament in the world, the World Cup.
