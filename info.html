History of the World's Favorite Sport:

Source: https://www.thetimes.com/sport/football/article/a-stitch-in-time-how-the-england-shirt-evolved-n9twpxxj7

Football as we know it has been around for over 250 years, but originally it was invented in China, called Cuju(蹴鞠).
Football was invented by Ebenezer Cobb Morley, who created the first club, Barnes Football Club, in 1862.
Before them, Sheffield FC, which is the oldest football club recognised by FIFA, and it was founded on October 24, 1857.
The first international game of football was played by England and Scotland, on November 30th, 1872, in Glasgow, Scotland, the score ended 0-0.
